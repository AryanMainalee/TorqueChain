    What is EMI & Vehicle Loan Documentation in Nepal

    Published: July 24, 2025 | EMI Explained for Nepali Buyers

    Planning to buy a vehicle in Nepal through installments? Here's what EMI means and what you need to prepare.

    What is EMI?

    EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment — a fixed amount you pay every month to repay your loan, which includes both principal and interest.

    Types of Vehicle Loans Available

    • Two-Wheelers: Scooters, bikes, and e-bikes
    • Four-Wheelers: Hatchbacks, SUVs, EVs, commercial vehicles

    Required Documents for EMI Financing

    • Citizenship certificate (copy)
    • Bank statement (6 months)
    • Salary slip / business proof
    • PAN card (for large loans)
    • Dealer quotation

    Key Considerations

    • Always confirm interest rate type: fixed or floating
    • Ask for an amortization schedule
    • Clarify prepayment options and penalties
