How Interest Rates Work: What Every Nepali Loan Taker Should Know

Published: July 24, 2025 | Personal Finance Basics

Understanding how interest rates work is key to making smart borrowing decisions in Nepal. Hereâ€™s a breakdown of how interest affects your loans:

1. Fixed vs Floating Interest Rates

  • Fixed: Remains constant during the loan term. Predictable but slightly higher.
  • Floating: Changes with bank base rates (NRB guidelines). May reduce over time.

2. What Affects Interest Rates in Nepal?

Banks in Nepal decide loan interest based on:

  • Your credit history
  • Loan type (vehicle, housing, etc.)
  • Income stability and repayment capacity
  • Current monetary policy by Nepal Rastra Bank

3. Importance of Interest Rate Comparison

Even a 1% difference in rates can mean thousands in extra payment over time. Always compare rates across banks.

4. Tips to Get Lower Interest Rates

  • Improve your credit score
  • Maintain consistent income records
  • Provide collateral if applicable
  • Negotiate terms with your bank

Stay updated with the Nepal Rastra Bank website for policy changes.
