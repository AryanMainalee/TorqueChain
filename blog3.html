    Car Loan Checklist in Nepal: Everything You Need to Apply

    Published: July 24, 2025 | Updated Vehicle Finance Guide

    Planning to buy a car on loan in Nepal? Here’s your complete checklist to avoid delays and confusion during the application process.

    1. Required Documents

    • Copy of citizenship certificate
    • Recent passport-size photographs
    • Salary slip / 6 months' bank statement
    • Vehicle quotation from the showroom
    • PAN card (mandatory for loans above Rs. 500,000)

    2. Loan Eligibility Criteria

    • Age 21–60 years
    • Stable job or business income
    • Clean repayment history

    3. Additional Costs to Consider

    • Processing fee: typically 0.5–1%
    • Vehicle insurance (mandatory)
    • Loan EMI insurance (optional)

    4. Helpful Tips

    • Use an EMI calculator to plan better
    • Get pre-approved to speed up the process
    • Always review the loan terms before signing
